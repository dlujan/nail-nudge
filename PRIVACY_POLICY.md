# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 2026

Thank you for using **Nail Nudge** ("the App").

Your privacy is important. Nail Nudge was designed with privacy as a core principle. The app performs all detection locally on your Mac and does not collect or transmit your personal information.

## Information We Collect

Nail Nudge does **not** collect, store, or transmit personal information.

The app does **not**:

- Create an account
- Require sign in
- Collect names or email addresses
- Track your activity
- Use analytics
- Store photos or videos
- Upload camera data to the internet

## Camera Access

Nail Nudge requires access to your Mac's camera in order to detect hand-to-mouth movement.

Camera frames are processed **entirely on your device** in real time. Images and video are **never saved, recorded, or transmitted** outside your Mac.

## Notifications

If you enable notifications, Nail Nudge uses macOS notifications to remind you when nail biting is detected.

These notifications are generated locally on your device and are never shared with us.

## Third-Party Services

Nail Nudge does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking technologies.

## Data Security

Because your data never leaves your device, there is no cloud storage or remote database containing your information.

## Children's Privacy

Nail Nudge does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
