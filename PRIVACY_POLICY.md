# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 2026

Thank you for using **Nail Nudge** ("the App").

Your privacy is important. Nail Nudge was designed with privacy as a core principle. The app performs all detection locally on your Mac and does not collect or transmit your personal information.

## Information We Collect

Nail Nudge does **not** collect, store, or transmit personal information.

The app does **not**:

- Create an account
- Require sign in
- Collect names or email addresses
- Track your activity
- Use analytics
- Store photos or videos
- Upload camera data to the internet

## Camera Access and Face Data

Nail Nudge requires access to your Mac's camera in order to detect hand-to-mouth movement associated with nail biting.

The app processes live camera frames entirely on your device in real time. During this process, the app may temporarily analyze visual information that includes your face and hands solely for the purpose of detecting nail-biting behavior.

**Face data is never:**

- Stored or retained
- Recorded as photos or videos
- Uploaded to the internet
- Shared with us or any third party

Any face data exists only in your Mac's memory while a frame is being analyzed and is immediately discarded after processing. No biometric templates, facial recognition data, or face identifiers are created or stored.

## Notifications

If you enable notifications, Nail Nudge uses macOS notifications to remind you when nail biting is detected.

These notifications are generated locally on your device and are never shared with us.

## Third-Party Services

Nail Nudge does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, cloud processing, or tracking technologies.

No face data, camera data, or other personal information is shared with any third party.

## Data Storage and Retention

Nail Nudge does not store face data, camera frames, photos, videos, or biometric information.

Because all processing occurs locally on your device, face data is retained only for the brief moment required to analyze each live camera frame and is immediately discarded afterward. No face data is saved to disk, uploaded to servers, or backed up.

## Data Security

Because camera processing is performed entirely on your device and no personal information or face data leaves your Mac, there is no cloud storage or remote database containing your information.

## Children's Privacy

Nail Nudge does not knowingly collect personal information or face data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
